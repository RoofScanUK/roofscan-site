1. These terms

These terms apply to any residential drone roof inspection booked through roofscanuk.co.uk. By paying a deposit and confirming a booking, you agree to them.

2. The service

We carry out an external drone survey of your roof and provide a written condition report with photos, condition ratings, and a Schedule of Works, delivered as a PDF within 48 hours of your balance payment. This is a visual external assessment. It does not replace a RICS survey or a building inspection where one is legally or contractually required.

3. Booking and payment

Prices are based on property type, any add-ons you select, and your travel zone, and are shown in full before you book. A £50 deposit is taken through Stripe to confirm your date. The balance, which is the total price minus the £50 deposit, is due on completion of the inspection, before the report is released.

4. Cancellations and rescheduling

You can reschedule free of charge with at least 24 hours' notice. Cancelling or rescheduling with less than 24 hours' notice, or not being accessible on the day, means the £50 deposit is forfeited. If we need to reschedule, most commonly because of unsafe flying weather, there is no charge to you and your deposit carries over to the new date.

5. Access on the day

The survey is external and carried out by drone, so no interior access is needed and you do not need to be home. Please let us know at the time of booking about anything that could affect the flight, such as livestock, overhead wires, nearby restricted airspace, or dogs.

6. Weather and safety

Drone flights depend on the weather. We will not fly in conditions that breach CAA safety rules or put people or property at risk. If we reschedule for this reason, there is no cost to you.
