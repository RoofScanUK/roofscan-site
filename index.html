About RoofScan UK

Roof inspections done properly, from the air

Local, independent, and built on real construction experience.

Why we started

Traditional roof inspections mean scaffolding, ladders, cost, and risk — or a roofer climbing up and telling you what they found with no evidence to back it up. RoofScan UK was built to fix that. A drone captures every tile, ridge, and flashing in high resolution, and you get a full written report you can trust and act on. No scaffolding. No guesswork. No conflict of interest.

Grounded in the trade

RoofScan UK is run by Takhy Ndiaye Marrero, who brings hands-on construction experience and a background in property to every inspection. That means reports written by someone who understands what they're looking at — not just pretty pictures, but a genuine assessment of what's going on up there and what to do about it.

Independent by design

We don't do the repairs, and we don't recommend who does. That's deliberate. Because we have no stake in the work that follows, our reports are impartial — we tell you what we see, honestly, with no referral relationships to influence the findings.

Local to Sussex

We're based in Brighton and cover the whole of Sussex — from Chichester to Rye, the coast to the mid-Sussex villages. Being local means we know the area, the housing stock, and the weather that batters these roofs. And it means a real person answers when you call.
