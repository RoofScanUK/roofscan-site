RoofScan UK, based in Brighton, East Sussex, is the data controller for the personal information described in this policy.

What we collect

Your name, email address, phone number, property address, and the details of the inspection you book. When you pay, Stripe processes your card details directly. We never see or store your full card number.

Why we collect it

To confirm and deliver your booking, take payment, produce and send your report, and respond to enquiries. Trade account holders also use this information to log in to the trade portal, which works by emailing a one-time code rather than storing a password.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of trusted providers to run the business: Stripe for payments, GoCardless for trade subscription billing, Cal.com for scheduling, Airtable for job records, Google Workspace for email, and Xero for accounting. These providers only process your data to help us deliver the service. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Where your data is processed

Some of the providers above may process data outside the UK. Where this happens, it is covered by their own standard data protection safeguards.

How long we keep it

Job and payment records are kept for 6 years to meet UK accounting and tax obligations, then deleted. Enquiries that do not turn into a booking are deleted after 12 months.

Cookies

This site does not use non-essential tracking or advertising cookies.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask to see the personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, restrict how we use it, or object to certain processing. Email info@roofscanuk.co.uk to make a request. If you are not satisfied with our response, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Children

This service is not directed at anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always the one published on this page.
