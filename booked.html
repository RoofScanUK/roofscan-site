What happens next
1
Deposit payment
A £50 deposit link is on its way to your email. Once paid, your booking is fully confirmed and in the diary.
2
Day-before reminder
We'll email you the evening before your inspection with a reminder and any on-the-day information.
3
The inspection
We'll arrive at the agreed time. The inspection takes around 45 minutes. You don't need to be present — but if you are, we'll brief you on arrival.
4
Balance payment & report
Within 48 hours of the inspection, we'll send a balance payment link. Once your payment is received, your full report with RoofScore is delivered within 48 hours.
