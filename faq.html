Booking
How do I book an inspection?+
Book directly through our online calendar at cal.com/roofscanuk/inspection. Choose a date and time, pay a £50 deposit to secure it, and you're confirmed.
Why do I need to pay a deposit?+
The £50 deposit secures your slot in the diary. It's deducted from your total inspection fee — you're not paying extra, just confirming the booking. The remaining balance is only due after your inspection is complete.
Can I cancel or reschedule?+
Yes, free of charge with at least 48 hours' notice — your deposit is refunded or transferred to a new date. Cancellations with less than 48 hours' notice may result in the deposit being retained.
Do you cover my area?+
We cover Brighton & Hove free of charge, with a small travel fee for wider Mid Sussex and Sussex addresses. Enter your postcode at booking and the correct price will show automatically.
On the day
Do I need to be home?+
No, you don't need to be present. If you are, we'll brief you on arrival before the drone takes off. Please keep pets indoors during the flight.
How long does the inspection take?+
Around 45 minutes on site, including setup and safety checks. The drone flight itself is usually much shorter.
What happens if it's raining or too windy?+
We won't fly in unsafe conditions — this protects the quality of your inspection as much as the equipment. If your slot needs to move due to weather, we'll offer the next available date at no extra cost, and your deposit carries over automatically.
Is it safe? Will anyone be walking on my roof?+
Nobody walks on your roof at any point. The entire inspection is external and aerial, flown by a CAA-licensed pilot with full public liability insurance. It's safer than a ladder-based inspection for everyone involved.
What if my property has trees or obstructions?+
In most cases this isn't a problem — we can manoeuvre around obstructions to get a clear view of every roof element. If access is genuinely restricted we'll let you know on arrival before proceeding.
Payment
When do I pay the rest of the fee?+
Once your inspection is complete and your report is ready, we'll send a secure payment link for the remaining balance. Your report is released once payment is received.
What payment methods do you accept?+
All major debit and credit cards via our secure Stripe checkout. No cash or cheques on the day.
Is my payment secure?+
Yes — all payments are processed through Stripe, a leading global payment provider. We never see or store your card details.
Your report
How long until I get my report?+
Within 48 hours of your balance payment being received (excluding weekends and bank holidays). Your inspection findings are ready as soon as payment clears, so paying promptly means a faster report.
What's a RoofScore?+
A RoofScore is a condition rating out of 100, calculated from every element we inspect — coverings, chimneys, rainwater goods and more. It gives you an instant snapshot of overall roof health, alongside the full written findings.
Where can I access my report?+
Your report is emailed to you directly, and also saved permanently in your personal portal at roofscanuk.co.uk/my-roof — log in any time with the email you booked with to see your full inspection history.
Is a drone inspection as good as a full survey?+
A drone inspection gives a thorough external visual assessment — it's not a replacement for a structural survey. If our findings suggest a deeper structural concern, we'll always recommend you consult a chartered surveyor.
Can I share my report with an estate agent or buyer?+
Yes, once you've paid in full the report is yours to share as you wish — with agents, buyers, contractors, or your insurer.
Trade accounts
Do you work with letting and estate agents?+
Yes — we offer Trade Accounts for agents, property managers and roofing contractors, with volume-based discounts and monthly invoicing. Email info@roofscanuk.co.uk to set one up.
How does billing work for Trade Accounts?+
Trade Account jobs are invoiced monthly rather than paid individually. Reports are delivered as soon as they're ready — you're not waiting on payment per job.
Who receives the report for a trade job?+
Both the agent's Trade Account portal and the property owner or tenant's email, based on the client email address entered at job submission.
